# Code of Conduct

## Our standard

The Meterless community is welcoming, respectful, and focused on building. We expect everyone participating in this repo (issues, pull requests, discussions, hackathons) to:

- Be respectful and constructive
- Assume good faith
- Keep criticism about the work, not the person
- Welcome newcomers and first-time contributors

Harassment, discrimination, and personal attacks are not tolerated.

## Enforcement

Report conduct issues to **sam@thedataplant.com**. Maintainers may remove comments, close threads, or ban contributors who violate this code.
